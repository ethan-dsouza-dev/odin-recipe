Pork Dumplings

Ingredients

  • Napa Cabbage
  • Ground pork
  • Scallions
  • Cilantro
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger
  • Sesame oil

Description

Whether you’re celebrating the Lunar New Year with friends this week or stocking your freezer for a busy month ahead, these dumplings should definitely be on your list. Here’s a step-by-step recipe to show you how to make them.

Steps

  1. Slice Cabbage and mix with salt
  2. Combine cabbage with rest of ingredients
  3. Place 1 Tbsp of filling on each dumpling wrapper
  4. Dampen edge of the wrapper with water
  5. Fold dumpling in half
  6. Make a pleat in either side
  7. Repeat with all the wrappers and filling

Instructions for cooking the dumplings

  1. Sear dumplings in a skillet
  2. Add 3 Tbsp of water to the pan
  3. Cover the pan and lower the heat
  4. Cook the dumplings for 3 to 5 minutes
  5. Transfer to a plate and serve
