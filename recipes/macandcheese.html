Mac & Cheese

Description

This old-fashioned macaroni and cheese is the easiest and best way to make this timeless classic. It’s cheesy, it’s quick, and it’s downright delicious.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups elbow macaroni, cooked
  • 2 TableSpoons butter
  • 11/2 cups shredded mild cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease a 1.5-quart baking dish with butter or non-stick spray.
  2. Add cooked macaroni, butter, cheese, salt, and pepper to the greased dish.
  3. Whisk together eggs and milk in a bowl. Pour the mixture over the macaroni.
  4. Bake for 40 to 50 minutes. Serve and enjoy!
